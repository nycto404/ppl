Formulas and Rules of Thumb

  • Load Factor = 1/cos(bank °)
  • Vstall bank = Vstall * sqrt(load factor)
  • TOD [NM] = (Current Altitude - Desired Altitude) / 1000 * 3
  • +27ft for every hPa increase
  • -2K for every 1000ft increase
  • IAS + 2% for every 1000ft increase. TAS is compensated also for temperature, IAS is not.

Speeds

  • Vrotate 50 kts
  • Vx 60 kts
  • Vy 67 kts
  • Vbest glide 60 kts
  • Vcruise climb 70 kts
  • Vbase 65 kts
  • Vfinal 60 kts
  • Note Wind increments on Final: for wind ≥10 kts, add 1/3 of headwind comp or full wind gust spread, whichever is higher, to Vfinal.

Emergency Checklist

Engine problem during take of roll

  1. Power idle
  2. Brakes as required
  3. Full stop
  4. Flaps up Optional
  5. Mixture cutoff Optional
  6. Ignition off Optional
  7. Master switch off Optional

Engine problem after take off

  1. Nose down, Vbestglide 60 kt
  2. Land within 30° left/right
  3. No turnaround below 1000 ft AGL
  4. Mixture cutoff
  5. Shut off valve close
  6. Ignition off
  7. Flaps as required
  8. Master switch off

Airwork Maneuvers

HASELL

A good way to make sure the plane and surroundings are prepared is HASELL

  • Height - Height above ground is sufficient
  • Airframe - Flaps up, doors and windows closed
  • Safety - Seatbelts fastened
  • Engine - Check engine parameters
  • Location - Suitable area for emergency landing
  • Lookout - Clear of traffic

Approach to stall in clean config

  1. Lookout / HASELL
  2. Approach check (Carb heat)
  3. PWR idle
  4. Defend the altitude by pitch
  5. When approaching stall -> Nose down, Go around (Full PWR, carb heat off)
  6. Climb check

Approach to stall in approach config (Flaps 20°)

  1. HASELL
  2. Approach check (Carb heat)
  3. Abeam threshold, Flaps 10°
  4. Approaching glide, 1500 RPM, Flaps 20°, Nose down
  5. Lookout, turn left
  6. When stalling, Nose down, Go around, Wings level
  7. Climb check

Recover from spiral dive

  1. Throttle idle
  2. Level wings
  3. Nose level
  4. Throttle full
  5. Climb check

Steep turn

  1. Lookout / HASELL
  2. Roll into 45° bank, increase PWR to maintain altitude
  3. Maintain altitude and airspeed
  4. Roll out 20° before desired heading
  5. Reduce PWR to cruise

Power idle approach

  1. Vbest glide: 60 kt
  2. Fly best heading (look for close and suitable spot to land, key position 1000 ft AGL & 1NM distance to threshold)
  3. Approach Check + Magnetos + Primer secured
  4. At key position - Flaps 10°
  5. Aim for the middle of the runway, if landing success is assured, proceed with flaps and aim for normal aiming point

Zero flaps approach

PWR idle when approaching glide, base and final speeds 5 kt higher than usual!

  1. Approaching glide - PWR idle
  2. Base 70 kt
  3. Final 65 kt
